Hello everyone!

So, Hel from Metropolis 1927, reimagined by me in the likeness of Marilyn Monroe, visits a beauty salon located in a crypt. Meanwhile, a spark ignites between her and the worker... and not just one! Those feet will be polished with a circular saw.

This is the first issue of the Spirits Show comic series, and due to its short length, you could call it a pilot episode.
